Wonderful citis in Kanagawa!
Check out this ramen museum in Yokohama! It showcases good old Japan from the year 1958. You can try out many types of ramens as well as learn the hisotry an culture of ramen at the same time!
Welcome to the enchanting island of street food and coastal delights! Indulge in delicious Kaisendon and savory seafood skewers while basking in the breathtaking views of the ocean from atop Enoshima. Discover a world where every bite is a journey to relaxation and satisfaction!
Hakone is famous for its stunning natural beauty, featuring picturesque Lake Ashi with possible views of Mt. Fuji. The city is also renowned for its relaxing hot springs and captivating art museums, offering a delightful blend of nature and culture for visitors to enjoy.
A captivating blend of tradition and modernity! Explore histric sites like Odawara Castle, savor local delicacies such as ell bowl or "sakura shrimp", and immerse yourself in the city's vibrant culture!
